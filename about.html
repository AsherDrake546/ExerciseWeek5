Our Story

A family tradition rooted in the flavors of the Dominican Republic, now serving the Kissimmee community.

From the Island to Your Table

Mangu Mountain is a family-owned Dominican restaurant based out of Kissimmee, Florida, serving the comfort food we grew up on — mangu, rice and beans, tostones, beef stew, and all the classic dishes that make Dominican cuisine so special.

The restaurant is led by Antonio Paulino, a second-generation Dominican-American chef with a deep connection to his roots and a passion for sharing Dominican food with the world. Growing up in a Dominican household, Antonio learned to cook alongside his family, where every meal was made from scratch and every recipe told a story. Those experiences shaped his belief that Dominican cuisine deserves a wider stage.

Antonio's mission with Mangu Mountain is simple: to bring authentic Dominican comfort food to more people and help it gain the recognition it deserves. Every dish on our menu reflects that goal — traditional recipes prepared with care, real ingredients, and the same love you'd find in a Dominican family kitchen.
